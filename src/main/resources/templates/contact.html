Description

The home-like café at Rantzausgade offers charm and personality during weekdays as well as in the weekend.
We offer delicious homemade food from our Bistro next door.
We offer everything from brunch, sandwiches, burgers and salat.
We offer beers, wine and drinks from our wide selection of beverages.
We also offer soft-drinks and a very well made cup of coffee (refill on regular black coffee only 10 dkk).
Tjili Pop is both a café and a pub. It's the kind of place where you greet complete strangers and find your way to the bar due to the homely atmosphere.
You'll find both regular customers, students and people having a great time with friends.
If you are interested in hosting a get together, a party or dining with your friends, please do not hesitate to call us at +45 35 35 90 20.

Live music

"Speake's Corner" songwriter showcase every Wednesday from 8.00-10.30 pm (free entry). Usually 4 acts playing 25-30 min. followed by a short open mic. session. Weekly updated programme at our facebook wall. Musical host & sound engineer: Jacob Speake www.facebook.com/jacobspeakemusic Skriv til jacobspeake@hotmail.com hvis du vil spille et 25 min. sæt til vores sangskriver-aften om onsdagen. Til open mic. kan man som udgangspunkt spille 1-2 numre, hvis man møder op ved 21-tiden og skriver sig på listen.
